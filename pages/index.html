Aberdeen Traffic and Air Quality Viewer

Welcome to the Aberdeen Traffic and Air Quality Viewer, here you will be able to explore the traffic and air quality in the city of Aberdeen at 8 locations during 2019 and 2020. This will allow you to be able to see the impact in which the initial COVID-19 lockdown in March 2019 had on the traffic flow through the city.

Aberdeen Traffic Facts

During 2019 there was an estimated 963.3 million miles traveled through the city of Aberdeen with 773 million of these miles done by cars and taxi's alone. During 2020 with the COVID-19 lockdowns this significantly affected the miles traveled throuhgout the UK and in Abderdeen it decreased by around 211 million miles.


Aberdeen Air Quality

The air quality in the city of Aberdeen is curerntly has a Air Qualitiy Index of 58 out of 500 (500 being the worst). This is classes Aberdeen as having moderate air quality which could effect a small group of individuals with certain health problems.
