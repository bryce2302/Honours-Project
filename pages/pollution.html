What is Carbon Footprint?

Carbon foorprint are the gasses which have been emited through the cosumption and production od any goods and services. Carbon footprint is a concept which is used to quantify what activity an activity has on climate change.

How to Reduce your Carbon Footprint

There are a number of things which individuals can do to help reduce your own carbon footprint. These can range from large changes to small everyday changes and if everyone did their bit, we could help to reduce our emissions.

- Limit meat consumption

- Avoid waste by only buying what you need

- Reuse clothes

- Cycle or use public transport

- Recycle your watse as much as possible
